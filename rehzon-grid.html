01

Package-Integrated Sensing

Simultaneously detects temperature excursion, tamper, spoilage, and fill-state from a single sensor element embedded in packaging. No batteries. No wires. External to container. Mass-producible.

02

Battery-Free Thermal Verification

Verifies thermal exposure wirelessly via dielectric change measurement. Damage tolerant. Flexible form factor. Tunable temperature range. No electrical connections required.

03

Material Anomaly Detection

Detects surface and subsurface anomalies, including hidden damage, moisture intrusion, and material-state changes invisible to surface inspection or standard logging.

04

Passive Checkpoint Identity

Long-range and wide-coverage passive identity at custody transfer points without GPS and without tag power. Both range and coverage simultaneously, which conventional RFID cannot deliver.

05

Anomaly-Aware AI

Learns nominal behavior from archived sensor data and flags real-time deviation without requiring examples of failure. Active alerting before compromised materiel reaches the next checkpoint.

06

Governed Provenance

Every scan, every pass/fail, and every anomaly flag flows into a governed record with full lineage and cross-domain relationships for decision-grade planning.

Pilot Program

Controlled Validation.

Rehzon Grid is preparing controlled validation pilots with regulated logistics and defense partners. The objective is to validate sensing accuracy, checkpoint reliability, and custody workflow integration under real operating conditions.

Detection Sensitivity

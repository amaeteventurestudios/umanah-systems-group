Spattio: Giving AI Eyes, Memory & a Map

Watch how Spattio connects AI agents to real-world spatial and temporal data streams, turning smart language models into context-aware systems.

The Problem

Why Spattio?

LLMs are smart, but blind. They can write your email, but they can't answer questions grounded in real places and real times.

Questions like these remain out of reach for standard AI systems today:

When did shipping delays spike in Amazon's SoCal network between May 6–9, 2025?

When did air quality drop below safe levels in Denver during the third week of January 2024?

Where were EV chargers offline in Texas on Tuesday, March 12th, 2024?

Which neighborhoods in Santa Rosa were evacuated first during the wildfire on August 17th, 2023?

Where did rainfall exceed 200mm in Lagos between April 1st and June 30th, 2024?

When did port congestion in Singapore trigger rerouting from June 10–15, 2025?

The Gap

LLMs Are Powerful, But Blind to Time, Location, and Context

As OpenAI themselves acknowledge: AI can make mistakes. Check important info. That's fine for fiction, but not for climate, logistics, infrastructure, or business ops.

Without Spattio

Smart words, blind world

Standard LLMs and AI copilots lack real-time awareness of where and when events occur. They reason from training data, not from live, located, timestamped facts. The result: confident-sounding answers that miss what actually happened.

With Spattio

Real-time awareness of where and when

Spattio gives AI real-time awareness of where and when things happen. Your copilots make decisions based on facts, not guesses, grounded in live environmental and geospatial data at the moment it matters.
